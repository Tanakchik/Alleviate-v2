Welcome To Alleviate Prison Leavers Re-entry Program

is an organisation which started operations in the Province of Masvingo Zimbabwe on the 15th of July 2022. We are a team comprising of Legal Practitioners, Police Officers, Correctional
Officers, a Journalist and Social workers. The team has been carefully assigned to successfully deal with issues pertaining to the side effects of the criminal justice.

    Alleviate Prison Leavers Re-entry program offers:

  • Transport Money from Prison
  • Permanent Housing Support
  • Employment Life skills workplace, Funding for projects and Workforce Development
  • Personal documents (birth certificates,IDs,Driver's licenses)
  • Ecocash and Bank Accounts
  • Legal registration of cell phone lines
  • Food Provisions
  • Clothing
  • Counselling
  • Clinical care and medication
  • Mental Health and Medication
  • Substance abuse treatment and monitoring
  • Education,Leisure,Hobbies and Positive Peers
  • Family reintegration Community reintegration

    Who is Responsible for the Returning Residents?

  • Rural and Urban development Agencies
  • Traditional Leaders
  • Landlords and real estate Agencies
  • Local Authorities
  • Workforce development Agencies
  • Prospective employers
  • Family and friends
  • The community at large
  • Clinics, Hospitals pharmacies and all private and government
  • Healthcare workers (Healthcare Providers)
  • Social work Agencies
  • The Zimbabwe Republic Police (ZRP)
  • Correctional Services
  • Church Leaders
  • Universities, Colleges and Schools
  • Agricultural Sector,Banks, Vehicle Inspectorate Department (VID)
  • Public Transportation System
  • The Legal Profession
  • The Government

Justice-impacted individuals:

include those who have been imprisoned or detained in a prison, any jail, immigration detention centre, juvenile detention centre, or any other place of confinement, those who have been charged but not found guilty, and those who have been arrested. It also includes their spouses, children or any other qualifying dependents.
